Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Roman pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolks
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Guanciale
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water.
  2. Fry the guanciale until crispy.
  3. Mix egg yolks and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine pasta, guanciale, and egg mixture.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve.
